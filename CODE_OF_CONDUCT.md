# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening a confidential issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) version 2.1.
